Review Of Breaking Bad

Cleyder Soto-Reyes

January 31st 2023

Breaking Bad, ever since i've first watched it has been a #1 pick in my list of favorite series. The odd thing is, at the time I first watched Breaking Bad normally it wouldn't have been my prefered genre. Regardless couldn't help but adore the characters and fall in love with the plot. Since then, very few shows i've watched have come close to Breaking Bad.
